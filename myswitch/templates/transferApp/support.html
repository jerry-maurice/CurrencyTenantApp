Change user password

To change your password, on the profile page, click on the arrow next to your name and select account. At the bottom of the page, click on the "Change password" button and you will be directed to a new page. Enter the required information.

Complete profile

Once the user has been given a username and a password, he or she still needs to complete their profile. In order to complete the profile, the user need to go to the account page. He or she needs to complete the missing fields and click on Update User

Log Out

To log out the user needs to click on the arrow next to their first name and then click on log out.

Create a transaction

To create a transaction, go to the navigation bar and click on transaction and fill out the inputs. the user also has the option to click on the transaction button on the homepage
